Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Shiven Vinod Khera and its subsidiaries (“we”, “us”, “our”), the developer of the 'Modernwave' mobile application as listed on the Google Play Store, are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how we collect, process, use, and protect your personal data through our products, services, and specifically our mobile application, 'Modernwave' (referred to as "the App").

2. Non-Login / Visitor Mode

If you use our services without creating an account (visitor mode) within the Modernwave App, we will only collect limited data necessary for the features you use, subject to your permission settings. For full services requiring an account, please complete registration.

3. Data We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, username, password, and profile picture.
  • Third-Party Login: Data authorized from services like Google, Facebook, etc.
  • Feedback and Support: Info shared during support interactions.

Information from App Permissions (if granted):

  • Location: For device pairing, geo-fencing, and smart device functions.
  • Camera: For QR scanning, taking photos/videos, video calls.
  • Photo/Video Library: To upload and share content in the app.
  • Microphone: For voice commands or recording audio.
  • Device Storage: For logs, crash reports, firmware updates, etc.

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device Data: MAC/IP address, OS version, device model, network type.
  • Usage Data: App usage, screens viewed, clicks, downloads, session duration.
  • Location Data: Used for automation and nearby device detection.
  • Logs: Collected for error tracking and improving app performance.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app and its features.
  • To communicate with you for support and service updates.
  • To personalize features or send marketing (with your consent).
  • To detect fraud, ensure security, and comply with the law.

5. Sharing Your Data

Your data may be transferred outside your country. We ensure protection through legal safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

7. Your Rights
